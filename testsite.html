Aug 2017-May 2019

Silver Chips Online

While I wasn't the biggest fan of writing, I decided to try out of the school newspaper. I first had to take the journalism class before trying of for the school paper. I fell in love with my journalism class. Journalistic writing was way better than the boring analysis essays we did in English class. I realized I could marry my nerdy love for technology with my passion for journalism by becoming a tech writer. Our online newspaper, Silver Chips Online , didn't have a dedicated technology section. So, I helped create the technology section in the newspaper and became the sole tech writer on the staff my junior year. In my senior year, I became the tech/blogpost editor and trained a junior mentee, Emmy, to become a new tech writer on the staff.
